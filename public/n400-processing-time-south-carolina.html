FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

I live in Columbia SC. Which USCIS office handles my N-400? +
Columbia is in Richland County in the Midlands region of South Carolina — served by the Charleston Field Office at 7 months. Verify at egov.uscis.gov/office-locator with your specific zip code.
I live in Greenville SC. Which USCIS office handles my N-400? +
Greenville is in Upstate South Carolina — served by the Greer Field Office at 7.5 months. The Greer office handles Greenville, Spartanburg, Anderson, and surrounding Upstate counties.
Is there a USCIS office in Greenville SC or do I go to Charleston? +
Upstate SC applicants use the Greer Field Office — not Charleston and not Greenville city itself. Greer is located between Greenville and Spartanburg. The office handles Upstate SC and processes in 7.5 months as of April 2026.
